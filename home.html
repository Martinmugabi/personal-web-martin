Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about my services and expertise

What services do you offer?

I specialize in full-stack web development and graphic design services. My offerings include:

  • Custom website development
  • Responsive web design
  • UI/UX design
  • Frontend development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript)
  • Backend development (Node.js, Python)
  • Brand identity and logo design
  • Print and digital graphic design

What technologies are you proficient in?

My technical skills span across multiple technologies and tools:

Frontend:

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Vue.js, Bootstrap, Tailwind CSS

Backend:

Node.js, Express, Python, Django, REST APIs, MongoDB, MySQL

Design:

Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, XD, Figma, Canva

What is your typical project workflow?

I follow a structured 5-phase approach to ensure quality results:

  1. Discovery: Requirements gathering and planning
  2. Design: Wireframing and prototyping
  3. Development: Coding and implementation
  4. Testing: Quality assurance and revisions
  5. Delivery: Deployment and documentation

This process ensures clear communication and measurable milestones throughout the project.

How do you handle revisions and feedback?

I believe collaboration is key to successful projects. My revision policy includes:

  • 2-3 rounds of revisions included in most project quotes
  • Clear feedback channels through email and project management tools
  • 48-hour turnaround for minor revisions
  • Weekly progress updates on larger projects
  • Final approval before project delivery

I'm committed to making sure you're completely satisfied with the final product.
